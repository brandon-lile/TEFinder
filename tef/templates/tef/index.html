Sequence Search

How to Use

Use the TEFinder to find transposons in your DNA sequence. Simply enter your FASTA sequence in box below and hit enter.

  • Threshold and Distance allow you to control how precise the search is.
    • Threshold is the percentage of matches you would like over the distance specified.
    • Distance is the minimum total distance that we should consider a threshold for.
    • For example, 33 matches over a distance of 50 bases would be caught with a maximum of 66% threshold.
